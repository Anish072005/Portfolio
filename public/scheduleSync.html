How it works

Three steps to full coverage

01

Teacher applies for leave

Teacher submits a leave request with reason, dates, and subject details from their dashboard.

02

Admin approves & AI suggests

Admin approves the leave. AI instantly ranks available free teachers by best fit for each period.

03

Substitute confirms

Admin sends the request. Substitute teacher sees it on their dashboard and accepts or rejects.
